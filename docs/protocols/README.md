## Discipline

Protocols are versioned (v0.1, v0.2, …). Each carries:

- Endorsement Marker (provisional, internal).
- Inheritance from the canonical scoping document's non-prescription clause.
- Stance log.
- Procedural-falsification statement: which downstream claims are invalidated by failure to invoke the protocol correctly.

## Distinction from memos and sails

- **Protocols** are binding methodological infrastructure. They do not argue; they specify.
- **Memos** investigate threads and conjectures. They argue.
- **Sails** are interpretive essays under the T4 kill-criterion.

A protocol that begins to argue should be split: the argument graduates to a memo, the operational rules remain in the protocol.
