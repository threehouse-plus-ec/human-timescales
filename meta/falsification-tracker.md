- **Installed.** Date.
- **Claim.** What is at risk.
- **Source artifact.** Where the handle is defined.
- **Falsification condition.** What would weaken or kill the claim.
- **Target date.** Binding completion date for the test.
- **Status.** OPEN / IN-PROGRESS / WEAKENED / FALSIFIED / CONFIRMED / EXPIRED.
